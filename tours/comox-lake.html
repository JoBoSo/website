Trip Report

      Comox Lake is wedged between the mountains behind the Comox Valley. Its south shore is lined with rustic wood cabins. There are views of the lake from the road and lots of black bears, luscious greenery, and orange arbutus trees along the way. At sunset, there was a sharp shadow projected on to the mountains across from my campsite on some bluffs.
