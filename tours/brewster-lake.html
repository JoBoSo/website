Trip Report

      My tour of Brewster Lake and Campbell Lake on the logging roads to the west of Campbell River was about the camping. I watched beautiful sunsets and had warm fires on both nights of my tour. My campsite on Brewster Lake was one of my favorites. It was so islandy with swooping cedar trees and knotty wood. I had a fire on the beach while bats were flying out of the forest above me.
