Trip Report

      I biked from Tyhee Lake Provincial Park, through Houston, to Red Bluff Provincial Park on my first day and the landscape and towns reminded me of northern Ontario. I camped on Babine Lake at Red Bluff Provincial Park for a night. I took a different route along logging roads back to Tyhee Lake the next day and saw some better views of the Babine Mountains. A guy gave me a ride back to Telkwa High Road when I was about 30kms away from it because he saw a grizzly bear on the road ahead of me.
